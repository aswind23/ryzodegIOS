Healthy dietary tips
  • Eat a wide variety of healthy foods.
  • Balance the number of calories with your activity level.
  • Choose foods rich in whole grains, vegetables, fruits and fat-free/low-fat milk products.
  • Eat lean meats, such as poultry and fish, and beans for protein.
  • Limit the amount of saturated fat and salt.
  • Cut down on added sugars.
  • Keep to sensible amounts of alcohol.
  • Have a proper breakfast every day to boost your body’s ability to burn energy.
  • Space your eating throughout the day and don’t skip meals.
